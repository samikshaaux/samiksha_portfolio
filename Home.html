How it started
Getting into design wasn’t something I had planned from the beginning. It happened gradually through small moments where I felt curious, frustrated, or just wanted to fix something that didn’t feel right. At first, I was drawn to the visual side of things. But over time, I started paying more attention to how things work the systems behind the screens, the decisions that shape how people interact, and how a small change can improve the whole experience.

What I Care About
I believe good design isn't just how it looks it’s how it works. I care about logic, structure, and designing flows that feel natural, not forced. When users don’t have to think twice, that’s good design.
